Buttermilk Pancakes


image from flickr

Description

A simple recipe that makes a dozen 5 inch pancakes in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Beat buttermilk, milk, eggs and melted butter into a seperate bowl.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan with medium-high heat.
  4. Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture. Use a spoon or fork to mix until its blended well but a little lumpy.
  5. Pour the batter onto the heated griddle using about 1/2 cup for each pancake.
  6. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes until bubbles apear on the surface. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
